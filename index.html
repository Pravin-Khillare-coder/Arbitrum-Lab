Why Ethereum Needs Layer 2

Ethereum is powerful but becomes slow and expensive when many users interact at once. Layer 2 solutions solve this by processing transactions off the main chain.

What is Arbitrum?

Arbitrum is a Layer 2 scaling solution that runs on top of Ethereum. It increases speed and reduces transaction costs while still using Ethereum’s security.

Real-World Benefit

Users can send transactions faster and pay much lower gas fees compared to Ethereum mainnet, making Web3 applications more practical for everyday use.
